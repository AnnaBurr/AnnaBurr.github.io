Cute but armed

This is a collection of my rarest bird sightings

These are highly evolved birds, and you must use caution when approaching.

The ten-fingered sipper

The Ten-Fingered Sipper can only be spotted from 7-8 am when it awakes for its morning coffee. After they have their coffee, they politely excuse themselves and go back to their nest.

The Tough winged burly back

I found this bird doing pull ups on a branch. If you get to close you may be in for a fight.

The intellectual red tail

This bird is commonly found reading and is endangered due to predators finding them. When predators do find them, they cannot put down the book before they finish their chapter. They cannot read at night because they do not have nightlights.
